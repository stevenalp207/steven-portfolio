## üöú RoboMission Senior Challenge

The **RoboMission Senior** category focuses on building and programming a fully autonomous robot capable of completing a series of missions related to **real-world scenarios**.

Key challenge elements included:

1. Autonomous navigation on a mission mat  
2. Precise interaction with mission models  
3. Task prioritization and route optimization  
4. Time-efficient execution within competition limits  
5. Consistent performance across multiple runs
